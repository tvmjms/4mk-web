## ðŸ’µ **Revenue Streams**

### **1. Freemium Model (60% of revenue potential)**

#### **Free Tier (Always Available)**
- Post unlimited needs
- Respond to offers
- Basic email notifications
- File uploads (up to 5 per need)
- Community access

#### **Premium Individual ($9.99/month)**
- **SMS notifications** (Twilio integration)
- **Priority listing** (needs show higher in feed)
- **Advanced analytics** (personal impact metrics)
- **Unlimited file uploads** (25MB per file)
- **Custom categories** (create specialized need types)
- **Export data** (personal records and receipts)

#### **Premium Organization ($49.99/month)**
- **Multiple administrators** (team management)
- **Branded community pages** (custom logos, colors)
- **Bulk posting tools** (import needs from spreadsheets)
- **Advanced reporting** (community impact analytics)
- **Custom integrations** (API access)
- **White-label option** (remove 4MK branding)

#### **Enterprise ($199.99/month+)**
- **Multi-community management** (manage multiple regions)
- **Advanced security** (SSO, custom authentication)
- **Dedicated support** (phone/video support)
- **Custom development** (feature requests)
- **Data ownership** (dedicated infrastructure)

---

### **2. Transaction-Based Revenue (25% of revenue potential)**

#### **Donation Processing (3% fee)**
- Users can donate to specific needs
- Platform takes 3% processing fee (covers Stripe fees + margin)
- 97% goes directly to need requester
- Optional feature - not required for platform use

#### **Service Marketplace (5-10% fee)**
- **Verified Service Providers**: Background-checked helpers for sensitive needs
- Platform facilitates connections, takes fee on completed services
- Examples: Pet care, home repairs, transportation services

#### **Corporate Sponsorship Program**
- Local businesses sponsor needs in their area
- Sponsored needs get highlighted placement
- $50-500 per sponsorship depending on visibility level

---

### **3. B2B Services (15% of revenue potential)**

#### **White-Label Solutions**
- License platform to other organizations
- Setup fee: $2,500-10,000
- Monthly licensing: $500-2,000
- Examples: United Way chapters, church networks, corporate volunteer programs

#### **Consulting & Training**
- Community building workshops: $2,500-5,000 per engagement
- Platform training sessions: $500-1,500 per session
- Custom implementation support: $150-250/hour

#### **Data & Analytics Services**
- Anonymized community insights for researchers
- Grant application support (demonstrate community impact)
- $1,000-5,000 per report/engagement
