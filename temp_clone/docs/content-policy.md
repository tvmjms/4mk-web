# 4MK Community Content Policy

## Our Mission
4MK is a community platform designed to help people connect and support each other through mutual aid and assistance. Our content policy ensures a safe, respectful, and helpful environment for all community members.

## ‚úÖ What We Encourage

### Community Support
- **Legitimate help requests**: Real needs for assistance with food, transportation, housing, medical care, childcare, employment, and other essential services
- **Genuine offers to help**: Sincere commitments to provide assistance to community members
- **Resource sharing**: Information about local services, programs, and opportunities
- **Success stories**: Updates on how the community has helped fulfill needs

### Respectful Communication
- **Kind and supportive language**: Encouraging words that build up community members
- **Clear and honest descriptions**: Accurate information about needs and offers
- **Constructive feedback**: Helpful suggestions and guidance
- **Cultural sensitivity**: Respectful interactions across diverse backgrounds

### Appropriate Documentation
- **Helpful photos**: Images that clarify needs or demonstrate assistance provided
- **Receipts and confirmations**: Documentation showing legitimate transactions
- **Progress updates**: Visual evidence of help being delivered
- **Resource materials**: Educational content that benefits the community

## ‚ùå What We Prohibit

### Explicit Content
- **Sexual content**: Any explicit sexual material, nudity, or sexually suggestive content
- **Inappropriate images**: Content that is not suitable for a family-friendly community platform

### Violence and Threats
- **Violent content**: Images, videos, or text depicting violence or harm
- **Threats or intimidation**: Content that threatens, harasses, or intimidates others
- **Self-harm content**: Material that promotes or depicts self-injury

### Illegal Activities
- **Illegal drugs**: References to, images of, or offers involving illegal substances
- **Weapons**: Content involving firearms, weapons, or dangerous items
- **Illegal services**: Offers or requests for illegal activities or services
- **Fraudulent content**: Scams, fake offers, or deceptive practices

### Hate Speech and Discrimination
- **Discriminatory language**: Content targeting individuals based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- **Hate symbols**: Images or references to hate groups or discriminatory organizations
- **Bullying or harassment**: Targeted attacks on individuals or groups

### Privacy Violations
- **Personal information**: Social Security numbers, credit card numbers, full addresses, or other sensitive data
- **Private communications**: Screenshots of private conversations without consent
- **Unauthorized photos**: Images of people without their permission

### Spam and Commercial Abuse
- **Promotional content**: Advertising, marketing, or commercial solicitations
- **Spam patterns**: Repetitive, irrelevant, or mass-produced content
- **Fake offers**: Deceptive or non-genuine assistance offers
- **Get-rich-quick schemes**: Financial scams or unrealistic money-making opportunities

### Inappropriate Images of Minors
- **Any inappropriate content involving children**: We have zero tolerance for any content that could exploit or endanger minors

## üìè Technical Limits

### File Size Limits
- **Images**: Maximum 10MB (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF)
- **Videos**: Maximum 50MB (MP4, WebM, MOV)
- **Documents**: Maximum 10MB (PDF)

### Text Limits
- **Need descriptions**: 500 characters maximum
- **Comments**: 500 characters maximum
- **Offer messages**: 200 characters maximum

### Upload Limits
- **Need creation**: Up to 3 media files per need
- **Comments**: Up to 5 media files per comment
- **Offer attachments**: Up to 10 files per offer
